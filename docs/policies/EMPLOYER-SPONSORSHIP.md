# Employer Training Sponsorship
## Elevate for Humanity Career and Training Institute

---

## Train Your Workforce. We Handle the Rest.

Elevate for Humanity partners with employers to train job-ready candidates in high-demand fields. You get skilled workers. We handle the training.

---

## Sponsorship Options

### Option 1: Cohort Sponsorship
Train a group of employees or new hires together.

- **Minimum:** 5 students per cohort
- **Discount:** Volume pricing available
- **Scheduling:** Flexible start dates for your team
- **Reporting:** Progress reports delivered to your HR team

### Option 2: Per-Seat Sponsorship
Sponsor individual employees for training.

- **Payment:** Invoice upon enrollment
- **Terms:** Net 30
- **Tracking:** Individual progress reports

### Option 3: Post-Hire Reimbursement
We train candidates. You hire them. You pay tuition after hire.

- **Structure:** Monthly payroll deduction or lump sum
- **Term:** Up to 12 months post-hire
- **Requirement:** Signed agreement before training begins

## Employer Benefits

**Tax Credits Available:**
- Work Opportunity Tax Credit (WOTC) — Up to $9,600 per hire
- On-the-Job Training (OJT) reimbursement — 50% wage subsidy
- Apprenticeship tax credits — $2,000 per apprentice (Indiana)

**We Help You Access:**
- WIOA employer incentives
- Workforce Ready Grant partnerships
- Registered Apprenticeship setup

## How It Works

1. **Contact Us** — Tell us your hiring needs
2. **Sign Agreement** — We formalize the sponsorship terms
3. **We Train** — Students complete the program
4. **You Hire** — Trained, credentialed candidates ready to work
5. **We Invoice** — Payment per your agreement terms

---

## Requirements

To sponsor training, employers must:

- [ ] Sign Employer Sponsorship Agreement
- [ ] Provide company information and billing contact
- [ ] Commit to payment terms in writing
- [ ] (For post-hire) Sign payroll deduction authorization

**No verbal agreements. All terms in writing.**
