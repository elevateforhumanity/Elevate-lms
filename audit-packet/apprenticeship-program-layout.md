## What Happens If Student Stops?

### Hour Validity
- **Hours completed remain on record** - they do NOT expire
- Hours are logged in RAPIDS (federal system) and our platform
- Can be used for re-enrollment or transfer

### Withdrawal Scenarios

| Scenario | Outcome |
|----------|---------|
| **Voluntary Withdrawal** | Submit written notice; hours preserved |
| **No Contact 30 Days** | Automatic withdrawal (abandonment) |
| **Payment Default** | Account suspension → eventual withdrawal |
| **Dismissal for Cause** | Immediate termination; hours still on record |

### Re-enrollment
- Re-enrollment is possible on a **case-by-case basis**
- Previous hours can be credited toward new enrollment
- May require new enrollment agreement and payment arrangement
- Subject to approval by program coordinator

## Transfer Hours

### Eligibility
- Prior barber school hours may be eligible for transfer
- Must submit official transcripts for evaluation
- **Maximum Transfer:** 750 hours (50% of total)

### Impact
- Transfer hours reduce **time-in-program**, NOT tuition
- Weekly payment amount may be recalculated based on remaining hours

## Milady (RTI) Requirements

### Theory Curriculum
- Online coursework via Milady platform
- Covers theory, safety, sanitation, state exam prep
- Approximately 150 hours of content
- Must complete all required modules with passing scores

### Topics Covered
- Hair cutting theory and techniques
- Shaving and facial hair services
- Sanitation and infection control
- Anatomy and physiology
- Chemistry of products
- Business and professional development
- State board exam preparation

## Practical Skills / Cuts Required

### Competency Checklist
Students must demonstrate proficiency in:

| Skill Category | Examples |
|----------------|----------|
| **Haircuts** | Fades, tapers, scissors work, clipper cuts |
| **Shaving** | Straight razor, facial hair shaping |
| **Client Services** | Consultation, communication |
| **Sanitation** | Tool disinfection, shop maintenance |
| **Business** | Client management, scheduling |

### Verification
- Skills verified by licensed barber mentor/supervisor
- Competency checklist must be completed before program completion
- Random audits may be conducted

## State Board Exam (PLA/IPLA)

### After Program Completion
1. **Receive Completion Certificate** from Elevate
2. **RAPIDS Completion** recorded in federal system
3. **Register with PSI Services** for exam scheduling
4. **Exam Fee:** Included in tuition ($50 value)

### Exam Components
| Exam | Format | Passing Score |
|------|--------|---------------|
| **Written** | 100 questions | 75% |
| **Practical** | Live demonstration | Pass/Fail |

### After Passing
- Submit application to Indiana IPLA
- Pay license fee (NOT included in tuition)
- Receive Indiana Barber License
- Must renew per IPLA requirements

## Hour Logging Requirements

### Submission Schedule
- Log hours **weekly** through student portal
- Deadline: **Sunday at 11:59 PM**
- Include: date, start time, end time, activity type

### Verification Process
1. Student submits hours
2. Supervisor/mentor reviews and approves
3. Rejected hours must be corrected and resubmitted
4. **Falsifying hours = immediate dismissal**

### What Gets Logged
| Type | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **RTI Hours** | Milady module completed (reference module name) |
| **OJT Hours** | Brief description of activities performed |

## Financial Summary

### Tuition
- **Total Program Fee:** $4,980 (flat fee)
- Same regardless of transfer hours or completion time

### Payment Structure
- **Setup Fee (35%):** $1,743 due at enrollment
- **Remaining Balance (65%):** $3,237 paid weekly

### Weekly Payment Calculation
```
Weekly Payment = $3,237 ÷ Weeks Remaining
```

| Hours/Week | Weeks | Weekly Payment |
|------------|-------|----------------|
| 40 | ~38 | ~$85/week |
| 30 | ~50 | ~$65/week |
| 25 | ~60 | ~$54/week |
