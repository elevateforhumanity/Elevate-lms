STUDENT ORIENTATION VIDEO SCRIPT — Elevate for Humanity
========================================================

Duration: 3-4 minutes
Tone: Warm, welcoming, professional, encouraging
Target: New students beginning their training journey

========================================================
FULL SCRIPT WITH SCENE DIRECTIONS
========================================================

[Scene 1 – Welcome / Founder or Staff Speaking to Camera]
Welcome to Elevate for Humanity.

If you're watching this, you've already taken the most important step—you decided to invest in yourself. That takes courage, and we're honored to be part of your journey.

[Scene 2 – Campus/Training Facility B-Roll]
At Elevate for Humanity, we do things differently. We believe that your zip code, your past, or your bank account should never determine your future. That's why our programs are designed to remove every barrier between you and a real career.

[Scene 3 – Students in Classroom + Online Learning]
Here's what you need to know about how our programs work:

First, your training is funded. Through partnerships with WIOA, the Workforce Ready Grant, JRI, and registered apprenticeships, most students pay nothing out of pocket. No loans. No debt. Just opportunity.

[Scene 4 – Hybrid Learning Demonstration]
Second, we meet you where you are. Our hybrid learning model means you can complete coursework online, attend hands-on labs in person, and move at a pace that fits your life. Whether you're a parent, working full-time, or starting fresh—we built this for you.

[Scene 5 – Hands-On Training / Lab Work]
Third, you'll learn by doing. Every program includes real, hands-on training. You won't just read about skills—you'll practice them until they become second nature. Our instructors are industry professionals who've done the work and want to help you succeed.

[Scene 6 – Career Services / Job Placement]
Fourth, we don't stop at graduation. Our career services team will help you build your resume, prepare for interviews, and connect with employers who are actively hiring. Many of our graduates have job offers before they even finish their program.

[Scene 7 – Student Success Stories / Testimonials]
You're joining a community of people who decided to change their lives. People who started exactly where you are right now. And they made it. You will too.

[Scene 8 – Expectations and Commitment]
Now, here's what we expect from you:

Show up. Whether it's online or in person, attendance matters. Your instructors are here to help, but they can't help if you're not present.

Ask questions. There are no dumb questions here. If you're confused, speak up. That's how you learn.

Stay committed. Some days will be hard. Some lessons will challenge you. But every successful graduate will tell you—it's worth it.

[Scene 9 – Support Resources]
If you ever feel stuck, reach out. Your student advisor is here to help with scheduling, funding questions, or anything else. Our support team is available through the student portal, by email, or by phone.

You are not alone in this.

[Scene 10 – Final Message / Call to Action]
This is your moment. Not tomorrow. Not someday. Right now.

You have everything you need to succeed. The training is here. The support is here. The opportunity is here.

All that's left is you showing up and doing the work.

Welcome to Elevate for Humanity. Let's build your future together.

PRODUCTION NOTES
========================================================

Recommended Voice: Warm, professional female or male voice
Pacing: Moderate, with pauses between sections
Background Music: Soft, inspiring instrumental (low volume)
Video Style: Mix of talking head, B-roll footage, student testimonials

Suggested Stock Footage:
- Diverse students in classroom settings
- Hands-on training (healthcare, trades, beauty)
- Computer/online learning
- Graduation ceremonies
- Job interviews / workplace scenes
- Indianapolis cityscape

Tools for Production:
- Voiceover: ElevenLabs, Murf.ai, WellSaid Labs, or professional talent
- Stock Video: Pexels, Artlist, Storyblocks
- Editing: DaVinci Resolve, Adobe Premiere, CapCut
