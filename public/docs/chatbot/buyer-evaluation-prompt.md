## Section 4: Fit Assessment

### Score Your Answers

**High fit indicators (Elevate likely a good match):**
- Manual tracking or basic LMS currently
- WIOA/FERPA compliance required
- 100+ learners expected
- Need certificates and employer connections
- Timeline within 90 days
- Budget $10,000+

**Lower fit indicators (may need different solution):**
- Under 50 learners total
- No compliance requirements
- Only need basic course hosting
- 18+ month timeline
- Budget under $5,000

## Section 5: Questions to Ask Any Vendor

Use these questions when evaluating Elevate or any alternative:

1. **Implementation**
   - What is the typical implementation timeline?
   - What resources do we need to provide?
   - Is there a dedicated implementation manager?

2. **Compliance**
   - How do you handle [specific regulation] reporting?
   - Can we see a sample compliance report?
   - How quickly do you adapt to regulatory changes?

3. **Support**
   - What support is included vs. additional cost?
   - What are your response time commitments?
   - Do you provide staff training?

4. **Data**
   - Who owns our data?
   - How do we export data if we leave?
   - What is your data retention policy?

5. **Pricing**
   - What is included in the base price?
   - What costs extra?
   - How does pricing scale as we grow?

6. **References**
   - Can we speak with similar organizations?
   - What outcomes have they achieved?
   - How long have they been customers?
