# LMS Policies Manual

## Elevate LMS Platform Policies

### 1. Acceptable Use Policy

#### Purpose
This policy defines acceptable use of the Elevate LMS platform to ensure a safe, productive learning environment.

#### Scope
Applies to all users: students, instructors, administrators, and partners.

#### Guidelines

**Users SHALL:**
- Use the platform for legitimate educational purposes
- Maintain confidentiality of login credentials
- Respect intellectual property rights
- Report security concerns promptly
- Follow course-specific guidelines

**Users SHALL NOT:**
- Share account access with others
- Upload malicious content or malware
- Harass or discriminate against other users
- Attempt to bypass security controls
- Use the platform for commercial purposes without authorization

---

### 2. Data Privacy Policy

#### Data Collection
We collect only data necessary for:
- Account management
- Course delivery
- Progress tracking
- Platform improvement

#### Data Protection
- All data encrypted in transit and at rest
- Regular security audits
- Access controls and monitoring
- Compliance with FERPA, GDPR, and applicable laws

#### User Rights
- Access your personal data
- Request data correction
- Request data deletion
- Export your data

---

### 3. Academic Integrity Policy

#### Standards
All users must maintain academic honesty:
- Submit only original work
- Properly cite sources
- Do not share assessment answers
- Report integrity violations

#### Violations
Consequences may include:
- Warning
- Grade penalty
- Course failure
- Account suspension
- Permanent ban

---

### 4. Content Standards

#### Course Content
All course materials must:
- Be accurate and current
- Respect copyright laws
- Be accessible (WCAG 2.1 AA)
- Be free from discriminatory content

#### User-Generated Content
Forum posts, comments, and submissions must:
- Be relevant to the course
- Be respectful and professional
- Not contain personal attacks
- Not include spam or advertising

---

### 5. Technical Requirements

#### Supported Browsers
- Chrome (latest 2 versions)
- Firefox (latest 2 versions)
- Safari (latest 2 versions)
- Edge (latest 2 versions)

#### Minimum Requirements
- Stable internet connection (5+ Mbps)
- JavaScript enabled
- Cookies enabled
- Screen resolution: 1024x768 minimum

---

### 6. Support and Escalation

#### Getting Help
1. Check the Help Center
2. Contact course instructor
3. Submit support ticket
4. Email: support@elevateforhumanity.org

#### Response Times
- Critical issues: 4 hours
- High priority: 24 hours
- Normal: 48 hours
- Low priority: 5 business days
